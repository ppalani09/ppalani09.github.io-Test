Temperature vs. Northern Latitude

In this plot, we show city temperatures plotted against latitude values of cities in the Northern hemisphere. As latitude values increase, we are moving away from the equator and towards the North Pole.

With increasing latitude - that is, as move further away from the equator and towards the North Pole - city temperatures decrease. Since the cities queried were randomly selected from among Northern Hemisphere cities, we can definitively assert, with data, that temperature decreases as one moves further away from the equator in the Northern Hemisphere.
